    Development Boards

    Reference design platforms for developers, featuring rich interfaces and high performance.

    • ESP32-P4-Pi (Flagship) --- The Ultimate RISC-V MPU Board

      • Chip: P4 (400MHz Dual-Core) + C6 (WiFi 6)
      • Memory: 32MB PSRAM + 16MB Flash
      • Media: H.264 Encoding, HDMI / MIPI-DSI, Ethernet View Details

    • ESP32-P4C6-Core (SOM) --- High-Performance Mini Module (27x27mm)

      • Core: ESP32-P4 + ESP32-C6 (Dual-Chip)
      • Power: 400MHz RISC-V + Wi-Fi 6 & BLE 5
      • IOs: High-speed GPIOs for HMI & Edge AI View Details

    • ESP32-S3 Round Board --- 1.85" Smart Round Display Kit

      • Display: 1.85" IPS TFT (360x360) + Touch
      • Audio: Onboard Mic & Speaker for Voice AI
      • Sensor: 6-Axis IMU (Gyro + Accel) View Details
